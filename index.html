A Cozy House is a place for keeping homeless, lost and abandoned animals, mainly dogs and cats.

Shelters are a key component of animal welfare and have four main functions: prompt care and care for the animal, including alleviating suffering through veterinary care or euthanasia; long-term care for an animal that does not immediately find an old or new owner; efforts to reunite a lost animal with its previous owner; search for a new habitat or a new owner for a stray animal
